About Me

Hello! I'm Michal, a passionate front-end developer on a mission to craft exquisite web experiences.

My academic foundation was built at the Code Institute, and I'm currently diving deeper into the tech realm with a comprehensive traineeship at CDETB. This combination has equipped me with expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

Beyond formal education, I've enriched my skills through mentoring at DevMentor.pl and by securing certifications from reputable institutions like Microsoft. These accomplishments not only accentuate my technical prowess but also underscore my commitment to constant learning.

Outside the world of code, I'm an avid traveler, drawing parallels between exploring new lands and navigating the vast universe of web development: both are about embracing challenges, discovering, and continuous learning.

If you're seeking a developer with a balance of formal training, hands-on experience, and a relentless drive to explore and improve, I'd be thrilled to connect. Dive into my portfolio to see what I've been up to, and let's chat about how we can collaborate!
